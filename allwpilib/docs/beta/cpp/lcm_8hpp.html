Returns
the least common multiple between integers a and b using the representation

\[ \text{lcm}(a,b) = \dfrac{| a b |}{\text{gcd}(a,b)} \]

where \( \text{gcd}(a,b) \) denotes the greatest common divisor between \( a \) and \( b \).
