drivetrainPlantThe LinearSystem representing the robot's drivetrain. This system can be created with LinearSystemId::createDrivetrainVelocitySystem or LinearSystemId::identifyDrivetrainSystem.
trackWidthThe robot's track width.
driveMotorA DCMotor representing the left side of the drivetrain.
gearingRatioThe gearingRatio ratio of the left side, as output over input. This must be the same ratio as the ratio used to identify or create the drivetrainPlant.
wheelRadiusMetersThe radius of the wheels on the drivetrain, in meters.
